About Our Initiative

Embracing the Girl Child Initiative is a non-governmental organization in partnership with LinkedLegal Atorneys aimed at providing legal representation, counseling and rehabilitation of victims of sexual abuse and domestic violence; sensitizing the girl child on various laws protecting the girl child, pubety, menstrual hygiene, career choices, sex ducation. etc. and also the free distribution of sanitary towels.
